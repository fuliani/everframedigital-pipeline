# Company Profile

- **Name:** EverframeDigital
- **Platform:** Etsy shop — https://www.etsy.com/shop/EverframeDigital
- **Sector:** Digital downloads / home decor / AI-generated art
- **What we sell:** Digital wall art bundles designed for Samsung Frame TV "Art Mode" (also usable on any 16:9 display, digital photo frames, screensavers). Each bundle is ~99-101 unique 4K (3840x2160) JPG images in one cohesive style, delivered as ZIP file(s), instant digital download.
- **Product line (as of last count):** Coastal-Landscape, Minimalist-Line-Art, Modern-Abstract-Neutral, Neutral-Botanical (+ a second "Neutral Botanical II / 100 Collection"), Seasonal-Neutral, Vintage-Botanical, Everframe-100-Collection (mixed-style flagship bundle), Vintage-Scripture-Paintings (KJV Bible verses overlaid on vintage-style paintings), All-Seasons-100-Collection, Japandi-Minimalist, Desert-Southwest, Emerald-Green-Abstract-Female-10-Collection (stylized abstract portrait line art). Prices generally $8.99-$14.99.
- **Target audience:** Frame TV owners and home-decor-conscious buyers who want an affordable, curated, cohesive art collection instead of buying individual pieces. Values variety (getting ~100 pieces to rotate) and a specific aesthetic (muted/neutral palettes across most of the catalog).
- **Brand aesthetic:** Muted, neutral, calm, elegant — most bundles use soft/desaturated color palettes (sage, cream, taupe, warm neutrals) with a few intentional departures (Emerald-Green-Abstract-Female is a bolder illustrative style).
- **Tone of voice:** Warm, straightforward, honest. Listings always include an AI-disclosure line and never overclaim (exact counts, no promised "human artist refinement," no money-back-guarantee language since digital downloads are non-refundable/final sale).
- **Positioning:** Not competing purely on price — competing on cohesive, curated, ready-to-rotate collections (buy once, get ~100 pieces) vs. single-image listings.
- **Note:** Etsy blocks automated fetching of shop pages (403/bot protection), so this profile is built from direct project work rather than live-scraped shop data. Update manually if shop details drift from this description.

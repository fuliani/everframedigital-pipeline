# Privacy Policy — EverframeDigital Pinterest Automation

**Last updated:** 2026-07-19

This application ("the App") is a private, internal automation tool built and operated solely by EverframeDigital to manage our own Etsy shop's Pinterest presence.

## What this App does

The App connects to a single Pinterest Business account (owned and operated by EverframeDigital) via the official Pinterest API to:
- Create and publish pins featuring EverframeDigital's own product artwork
- Read basic account and board information needed to manage those pins

## What data we access

- Pinterest account credentials (OAuth access token) for the single connected EverframeDigital account only
- Pin and board data belonging to that account

We do not access, collect, or process data belonging to any other Pinterest user or account. This App is not distributed publicly and has no external end users — it exists solely to operate one business's own Pinterest presence.

## What we don't do

- We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.
- We do not collect analytics or tracking data from Pinterest visitors or pin viewers.
- We do not use Pinterest data for any purpose other than managing our own account's pins and boards.

## Data storage

OAuth tokens are stored locally and privately in our own systems, not shared with any third party, and used only to authenticate API requests on behalf of the EverframeDigital Pinterest account.
